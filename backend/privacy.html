Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 02, 2025

BJJ Progress ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by BJJ Progress.

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, update your profile, or use our services. This includes:

  • Account Information (Email address, Password)
  • Training Data (Dates, times, notes, techniques)
  • Profile Information (Belt rank, stripes)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Appwrite services. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

4. Account Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. You can do this directly within the application settings under "Delete Account".

5. Contact Us
